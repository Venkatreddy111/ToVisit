Mahabodhi Temple, Bodh Gaya

The Mahabodhi Temple is one of the holiest sites in Buddhism. It is where Gautama Buddha is said to have attained enlightenment.

Nalanda University Ruins

The ruins of Nalanda University, an ancient center of learning, offer a glimpse into India's rich intellectual heritage.

Vikramshila University

Vikramshila University, established in the 8th century, was a significant center for Buddhist studies and philosophy.

Rajgir

Rajgir, an ancient city, is associated with Buddha's teachings and is a site of historical and religious significance.

Sasaram - Tomb of Sher Shah Suri

The tomb of Sher Shah Suri in Sasaram showcases exquisite Indo-Islamic architecture and is a historical marvel.

Pawapuri

Pawapuri is a Jain pilgrimage site, believed to be the place where Lord Mahavira attained nirvana (liberation).

Vaishali

Vaishali is an ancient republic and a significant archaeological site associated with Buddha and Jainism.
